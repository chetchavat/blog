Ben and Jerry's Town Center Scooper

Sugar Land, Texas 2006 - 2007

I scooped ice cream.

I learned how to clean. I learned how to make everything: milkshakes, smoothies, waffle cones, sundaes. I learned that sometimes, adults don't listen to you because they don't want to. I later learned it was because I was serving them.

I learned that Baklava ice cream was gross, strawberry sorbet and peanut butter ice cream make a PB&J, and that friends will exploit you for free ice cream.

I was paid minimally, and I embraced tips.

Cherry Garcia is still pretty good.
